# Banned External Profile Language: Yaga Calls

To maintain the "Serious Risk-Aware" positioning and comply with trust standards, the following language is **strictly prohibited** on any official Yaga Calls profile, bio, or public communication.

## Prohibited Terms (Banned)
*   **Easy Money / Free Money**: Never imply that trading is low effort or guaranteed income.
*   **Guaranteed Profit / Returns**: No signal can guarantee financial outcomes.
*   **No Loss / Risk-Free**: All trading involves risk; claiming otherwise is a red flag.
*   **100% Accurate / Sure Shot**: Even the best setups can fail.
*   **Money Printing**: Avoid slang that trivializes financial risk.
*   **Join Before Pump / Next 100x**: Avoid FOMO-driven, pump-style urgency.
*   **Passive Income Guaranteed**: Trading is an active discipline with variable outcomes.
*   **Secret VIP Pumps**: Yaga Calls does not participate in pump-and-dump schemes.

## Prohibited Tone (Banned)
*   **Mocking**: Never mock users who missed a trade or used different analysis.
*   **Pure Profit Screenshots**: Never post a "green" screenshot without technical context, entry logic, or a risk disclaimer.
*   **Exaggerated Claims**: Avoid claiming "Everyone is making money" or "Students are printing."

## Correct Alternatives
| Prohibited Phrase | Approved Replacement |
| :--- | :--- |
| "Free money on this long" | "Structured long setup with defined invalidation" |
| "100% accurate signal" | "Historically high-conviction setup based on [Narrative/Technical]" |
| "Join before it pumps" | "Review entry zone before price expansion" |
| "Guaranteed profit" | "Targets defined as review areas for trade management" |
| "No loss strategy" | "Risk-managed approach with strict stop-loss logic" |

---
**Note:** These terms may only appear on the official website when used as examples of "Red Flags" for users to avoid in other groups.
